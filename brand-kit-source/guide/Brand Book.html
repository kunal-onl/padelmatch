01
LOGO MARK
DEFAULT · LETTER VARIANTS · ANIMATED
The PadelMatch mark is a top-down padel court rendered as a 6-cell glass-panel grid. Default identity: TL·lime + BR·blue diagonal — two players matched across the net. The mark also resolves into P and M letterforms drawn from the same court grammar.
02
WORDMARK — CASE STUDY
4 OPTIONS · PADEL·INK / MATCH·LIME
Four ways to set the brand name. All share the chosen color split: PADEL in ink, MATCH in lime. Unbounded display, weight 900, tracking −0.04em. Pick your favorite and lock it in.
03
WORDMARK — PADEL COLOR VARIANT
INK vs BLUE · MATCH ALWAYS LIME
Two color treatments for the recommended pm-split wordmark. PADEL · ink is the conservative default — high contrast, works everywhere. PADEL · blue brings the brand blue into the wordmark for a more saturated, energetic identity. MATCH stays lime in both — it's the active half of the "match".
04
WORDMARK — WEIGHT EXPLORATION
700 · 900 · MIX
Three weights of Unbounded applied to the recommended case. 900 is most assertive — recommended for primary use. 700 is the elegant alternative for editorial / print. Mix heavies the whole word block but leaves room to experiment.
05
WORDMARK — SPECIAL TREATMENTS
CLEAN · MINI-MARKS · COURT UNDERLINE
Three flavors to compare. Clean is the safe everyday form. Mini-marks replaces the literal P and M glyphs with miniature versions of the letter-marks — the most distinctive but only legible at hero scale. Court-grid underline binds the wordmark to the visual system with a single horizontal stroke.
06
LOCKUPS
HORIZONTAL · STACKED · MARK · WORDMARK · TAGLINE
The official lockup family. Horizontal is the default — use in app bars, headers, footers. Stacked for square placements (avatars, app store screenshots). Mark only at small sizes (favicon, navigation). Wordmark only in body copy or text-heavy contexts. Tagline lockup for marketing and brand moments.
